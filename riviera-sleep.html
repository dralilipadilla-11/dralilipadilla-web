In many countries, getting a proper sleep study takes 4–12 months. In Cancún, we can have your polysomnography scheduled within days, your evaluation done in one week, and a treatment plan in your hands before you fly home.

How it works

The three-phase program

From your first message to your last follow-up, we manage everything.

01

Remote pre-screening

Before you book your flights

  • Video consultation with Dr. Lili
  • Sleep history review and symptom scoring
  • Preliminary questionnaires (Epworth, STOP-BANG)
  • Trip planning guidance (dates, logistics, hospital)
  • Pre-authorization of diagnostics
02

In-person — Cancún

4–7 days, Hospital Galenia

  • Full polysomnography (PSG) study
  • ENT evaluation and nasal endoscopy
  • Drug-induced sleep endoscopy (if indicated)
  • CPAP titration or MAD fitting (if applicable)
  • Airway surgery with Dr. Jorge Barbachano (if indicated)
  • Myofunctional therapy session + RespiraGym training
03

Remote follow-up

After you return home

  • 30-day follow-up video call
  • 90-day results review with objective data
  • CPAP compliance analysis (if applicable)
  • Myofunctional therapy support via RespiraGym
  • Direct WhatsApp access during recovery

Comprehensive care

Everything under one roof

No referral chains. No waiting lists. One team, full picture.

◈

Polysomnography

Full overnight sleep study. Gold standard for diagnosing OSA, UARS, insomnia, parasomnias, and RLS. Report available within 24h.

◎

ENT Assessment

Nasal endoscopy, pharyngeal evaluation, airway anatomy review. Dr. Lili identifies structural contributors that CPAP alone won't fix.

◐

Airway Surgery

Uvulopalatopharyngoplasty, septoplasty, turbinoplasty, tonsillectomy. Dr. Barbachano performs these at Hospital Galenia — one of Mexico's top private hospitals.

⊕

CPAP & MAD

If CPAP is indicated: titration study included. Mandibular Advancement Device (MAD) assessment for mild-moderate OSA or CPAP-intolerant patients.

◑

Myofunctional Therapy

Oropharyngeal muscle exercises proven to reduce AHI by 50–62% in mild-moderate OSA. Taught in-person, continued remotely via RespiraGym.

○

Bilingual Coordination

Every appointment, report, and recommendation is delivered in English. Hospital liaison included. No translation barriers in your care.

S · E · R — Sleep · Equilibrium · Respiration

A team built
around your airway.

Sleep and breathing are the same system seen from different angles. We evaluate from the sleep laboratory, apply surgical correction when the data indicates it, and retrain what was lost. This is S·E·R.
